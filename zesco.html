Zambia Electric Supply Corporation ltd (Zesco)

Company Overview

Zesco Limited is a vertically integrated electricity utlilty, which generates, transmits, distributes and supplies electricity i Zambia
Core Ideology: Zesco's value system is explained in mission and vision statement.
Mission: Making it easy for people to live a better life.
Vision: To be the hub for electricity by 2025.

Brief History

ZESCO Limited was formed in 1970 after Zambia Electricity Supply Act was passed in parliament. it was in Zambezi district in the late 90's. The first form of electric production was coal which was used from time of origin to around 2015 when the hydro-electric project was in motion.
