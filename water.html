North-Western Water and Sewerage Company

Company Overview

The Water sector in Zambia has gone through a major transformation aimed at making it more responsive to the changing needs and concerns of the customersand the general public.
Core Functions: Zambezi community core functions are summed up as follows:

  • To abstract water from various water sources
  • To treat the water to meet Zambia bureau of standards specifications for drinking water
  • To deliver the treated water to customers
  • To collect and treat collected waste water by stabilising all pollutants to (ZEMA)standards
  • To return treated waste water supply to the aquatic environment.

    • Mission: To consistently and efficiently provide adequate, safe quality water and Sewerage services for the well being of our customers and the enviroment.
    Vision: To be a symbol of exellence in the water and sanitation industry.

    Project

    ZESCO Limited was formed in 1970 after Zambia Electricity Supply Act was passed in parliament. it was in Zambezi district in the late 90's. The first form of electric production was coal which was used from time of origin to around 2015 when the hydro-electric project was in motion.

    Projects

    The National Urban Water and Sanitation Programme(NUWSP) was a unified approach which the government of the republic of Zambia (GRZ) had adopted in order to improve the development of water supply and sanitation services in all urban and rural areas in the country. It was supported by the African Development Bank (AfDB).

    Project Goal

    To improve the provision of adequate safe and cost effective water and sanitation to urban & rural areas in the country in this case within Zambezi ward.
